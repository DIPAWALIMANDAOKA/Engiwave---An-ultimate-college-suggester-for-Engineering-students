Information about Branches

Embark on an extraordinary journey through the diverse realms of engineering, where possibilities are as vast as the universe.

Computer Science and Engineering

Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) is a dynamic and rapidly evolving field that encompasses the study of computer systems, algorithms, programming languages, data structures, artificial intelligence, software development etc.

Electrical Engineering

Electrical Engineering involves the study of electrical systems, circuits, and electronics. Graduates specialize in designing and managing power systems, communication networks, and electronic devices, contributing to advancements in technology and infrastructure.

Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical engineering involves designing and manufacturing mechanical systems for industries such as automotive and aerospace. Professionals in this field typically hold a bachelor's degree and work on diverse projects, including product design and research.

Civil Engineering

CE focuses on the design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure projects. Graduates are involved in creating and managing structures like buildings, bridges, and transportation systems, contributing to the development of sustainable and resilient communities.

Electronics and Communication

ECE emphasizes electronic circuits, communication systems, and signal processing. Graduates contribute to the design of communication devices, playing a crucial role in the advancement of technology.

Chemical Engineering

ChE applies chemical and physical principles to industrial processes. Graduates work in fields such as chemicals, pharmaceuticals, and materials, contributing to the development of new products, processes, and sustainable technologies.

Aerospace Engineering

Aerospace Engineering involves the design and development of aircraft and spacecraft. Graduates contribute to the exploration of space, advancements in aviation, and the development of cutting-edge aerospace technology.Deals with the design, development, testing, and production of aircraft and spacecraft.

Biotechnology Engineering

Biotechnology Engineering integrates biology and technology for applications in healthcare, agriculture, and the environment. Graduates contribute to the development of new medicines and innovative solutions in the field of biotechnology.

Information Technology

Information Technology (IT) involves the use of computers, networks, and software to store, retrieve, transmit, and manipulate data.IT professionals develop, implement, and manage technology solutions to support businesses & organizations.Similar to CSE but often with a more business-oriented focus, emphasizing the application of technology.

Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science focused on creating intelligent systems that can think like human. It involves developing algorithms for problem-solving. AI has diverse applications, from virtual assistants and image recognition to autonomous vehicles and healthcare.

Machine Learning

Machine Learning (ML) is a branch of artificial intelligence that involves the development of algorithms allowing systems to learn patterns from data and make predictions or decisions. Surface Coating Technology

Surface Coating Technology is a branch of engineering dedicated to applying protective or decorative coatings on materials. Engineers in this field strive to develop innovative coating solutions for diverse industries, contributing to the performance of various products and structures.

Food Technology

Food Technology in engineering involves the application of engineering principles to the production, processing, preservation, and packaging of food.This field integrates knowledge from chemical engineering, microbiology, and materials science to optimize and innovate food manufacturing processes methods and properties of polymers.

Plastic and Polymer Technology

Plastic and Polymer Technology focuses on the study and application of polymer materials, which are essential components in the production of plastics, rubbers, and various composite materials.

Paper and Polymer Technology

Paper and Polymer Technology combines the study of traditional with modern polymer science. This field involves developing and optimizing materials that integrate both paper and polymer components, leading to strength & durability. It plays a crucial role in industries.

Paper and Pulp Technology

Paper and Pulp Technology involves the efficient production of paper through processes like pulping and papermaking.The field plays a crucial role in producing a wide range of paper products for various applications, from packaging to printing.

Petrochemical Technology

Petrochemical technology engineering is a branch of engineering that deals with the design, operation, optimization, and management of processes and facilities involved in the production of petrochemicals.

Oil Technology

Oil and technology integration involves leveraging advanced technological solutions in the extraction, refining, and distribution processes within industry. This integration is pivotal for optimizing resource extraction and ensuring sustainable practices.

Robotics and Artificial Intelligence

Robotics and Artificial Intelligence (AI) are closely related branches within the field of engineering, computer science, and other disciplines to create intelligent systems capable of performing tasks autonomously.

Industrial IoT

The Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT) is a significant branch within the broader field of engineering, specifically focused on the integration of internet-connected devices and technologies in industrial processes.

Production Engineering

Production engineering focuses on optimizing manufacturing processes, implementing automation for cost-effectiveness. It involves material selection, contributing to efficient and sustainable production.

Automation and Robotics

Automation and robotics involve the use of technology to perform tasks without human intervention. Automation typically refers to the use of systems and software to control and streamline processes, while robotics involves the design and implementation of mechanical devices . Together, they enhance efficiency, precision, and safety across various industries.

Textile Engineering

Textile engineering is a branch of engineering that focuses on the design and production of fibers, fabrics, and textiles. It involves the application of principles from mechanical, chemical, and material engineering to create and improve textile materials. Their responsibilities also include quality control, production optimization.

Artificial intelligence and Data Science

Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Data Science are interdisciplinary branches within the engineering field that focus on leveraging advanced algorithms, computational methods, and data analyticts . Artificial intelligence and Data Science are transformative technologies with applications spanning healthcare, finance, manufacturing etc.
